Recipe to make milkshake

Description

Here's a simple recipe for a classic vanilla milkshake:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional, if you want an extra vanilla flavor)

Steps:

  1. Scoop the vanilla ice cream into a blender.
  2. Pour in the milk.
  3. Add the vanilla extract if desired.
  4. Blend the ingredients until smooth and creamy. You can adjust the consistency by adding more milk if you prefer a thinner milkshake.
  5. Once blended to your liking, pour the milkshake into glasses.
  6. Optional: You can top the milkshake with whipped cream, sprinkles, or a cherry for an extra treat.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious homemade vanilla milkshake!
