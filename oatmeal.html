Oatmeal Recipe

Description

Here's a basic recipe to make oatmeal:

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats (old-fashioned oats)
  • 2 cups water or milk (you can use any type of milk - cow's milk, almond milk, soy milk, etc.)
  • Pinch of salt (optional)
  • Optional toppings: honey, maple syrup, fresh or dried fruit, nuts, seeds, cinnamon, nutmeg, etc.

Steps:

  1. In a saucepan, bring the water or milk to a boil.
  2. Stir in the rolled oats and salt (if using).
  3. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for about 5 minutes or until the oats are tender and the mixture has thickened to your desired consistency. If you prefer a thicker oatmeal, simmer for a bit longer.
  4. Once the oatmeal is cooked, remove the saucepan from the heat.
  5. Serve the oatmeal hot in bowls.
  6. Add your favorite toppings such as honey, maple syrup, fresh or dried fruit, nuts, seeds, cinnamon, or nutmeg.
  7. Stir the toppings into the oatmeal and enjoy!
